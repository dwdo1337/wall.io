## Telegram — feature follow-up
the badge shows two numbers: average hold time and median hold time.

average can get skewed by one outlier trade. median is the honest middle — half the wallet's trades held longer, half shorter. when they're close, the wallet's behavior is consistent. when they're far apart, watch out — one or two huge holds are dragging the average up while most trades are quick flips.

press 1 / 3 / 7 / 0 to switch the lookback window without touching a menu. Esc closes the badge.

## Tone rules
- lead with the badge, not the company — this is a utility, not a platform
- never claim "median" is populated if it isn't in the actual UI (currently shows "—" — script bug, don't paper over it in real screenshots, only in illustrative mockups clearly marked as such)
- color meaning is fixed: green = 4h+ (holds), yellow = 1–4h (flips), red = under 1h (dumps fast) — don't invent different thresholds
- never say "alpha," "signals," "guaranteed" — this is a data tool, not a call service
- always mention local-first / no account when privacy comes up — it's a real differentiator, not filler
